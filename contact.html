You might be a 90s person if...

  • You know where, "You might be a..." saying came from.
  • You rushed home from school, practice, wherever to watch the Disney Afternoon.
  • Saturday mornings were for cartoons.
  • You wanted to swim in a pile of gold and wanted Launchpad McQuack to land one time safely.
  • Playing outdoors to the porch light came on.
  • You always went for the Physical Challenge and you know you would have won if you were ever on Double Dare. Also you knew all the words to the Nikelodeon theme song. Plus hearing "Happy, Happy, Joy Joy" made you happy.
  • Arguments over which system was better (Sega or Nintendo) broke out on the playground.
  • Jelly bands and ripped acid wash jeans were every where.
  • Your Gameboy went ever where.
  • You owned a digital pet.
  • You said "Sike!" at least once
  • Angelica always gave Tommy and his friends a hard time.
